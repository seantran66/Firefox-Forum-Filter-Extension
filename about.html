About Forum Filter

The Forum Filter Extension helps sift through the noise in On3 forum threads, aiming to provide users with a more informative and efficient a browsing experience.

This is accomplished by hiding posts that fall below a user-defined reaction threshold. Posts authored by whitelisted usernames are always shown, regardless of reaction count.

How It Works

Filtering is applied directly to the page content after it loads. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Privacy

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No information leaves your device.

Controls

You can enable or disable filtering at any time using the extension popup. Filtering rules can be adjusted in the Settings page.
